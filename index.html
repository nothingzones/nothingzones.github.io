WELCOME TO NOTHING SPACE COMMUNITY HUB

Nothing Space is an unofficial, community-driven resource hub built for enthusiasts of the Nothing™ technology ecosystem. Our goal is to provide a seamless experience for customizing and maintaining your Nothing Phone (1), Phone (2), and CMF devices.

OFFICIAL FIRMWARE REPOSITORY

We provide a direct mirror to official OTA (Over-The-Air) firmware zip files. This allows advanced users to manually update their Nothing OS version, unbrick devices, or switch between regional builds (Global, Europe, India) safely and quickly.

MODDING & TUTORIALS

Unlock the full potential of your device with our step-by-step guides. Whether you want to unlock your bootloader, root your phone via Magisk, or install custom ROMs, our library of tested tutorials makes the process safe and easy for everyone.

NOTHING APPS & PORTS

Experience the iconic dot-matrix design on any Android device. Download the latest APKs for the Nothing Launcher, Weather, and Recorder apps, along with the Nothing Icon Pack to transform your interface.

CUSTOMIZATION & TOOLS

Explore our exclusive gallery of high-resolution wallpapers optimized for OLED displays and the Glyph Interface. Download verified GCam (Google Camera) XML configuration files to enhance photography, and access our custom-built Ringtone Player.

ACCESSORIES & MERCH

Find the perfect match for your transparent back. We curate the best cases, skins, and tempered glass from top brands like Spigen and Gadgetshieldz, plus 3D-printable files for CMF accessories.

PHOTOGRAPHY SHOWCASE

See the world through the Nothing lens. Browse our "Shot on Nothing" gallery featuring the best community photos, macro shots, and long-exposure light painting experiments.

JOIN THE CONVERSATION

Don't explore the void alone. Join our active Telegram and Discord channels to troubleshoot issues, share your Glyph composer creations, and discuss the latest leaks and Nothing OS beta features with thousands of other members.

SERVICE CENTER LOCATOR

Find authorized service centers near you with our comprehensive database. Access contact details, timings, and direct map links for centers across India.

    Welcome to Nothing Space. We are a dedicated open-source resource hub built for enthusiasts of the Nothing™ technology ecosystem. Our mission is to bridge the gap between complex developer resources and the average user.

    OUR MISSION

    To provide a safe, ad-free (pop-up free), and direct download environment for Firmware, Mods, and Tools. We believe in the "Right to Repair" and "Device Customization" philosophies.

    EDITORIAL STANDARDS

    Reliability is our core value. To ensure user safety, every file hosted on Nothing Space undergoes a strict 3-step verification process:

    • Source Verification: We only mirror links from official OTA servers (android.googleapis.com) or trusted developers.
    • Security Scanning: All APKs are scanned via VirusTotal APIs before listing to ensure 0% malware.
    • Manual Testing: Our internal team tests UI mods on real hardware (Phone 1/2) to ensure stability.
