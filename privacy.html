PRIVACY POLICY

LAST UPDATED: JANUARY 2026

At Nothing Space (accessible from nothingzones.github.io), safeguarding the privacy of our visitors is one of our primary objectives. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Nothing Space and how we use it.

1. INFORMATION COLLECTION

Personal Data: We do not require you to create an account or provide personal details (like Name, Phone Number, Address) to access our downloads.
Voluntary Data: If you contact us via email, we will only use your email address to reply to your query. It will never be sold or shared.

2. LOG FILES (ANALYTICS)

Nothing Space follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The data collected includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, and referring/exit pages. This information is used for analyzing trends, administering the site, and preventing DDoS attacks.

3. GOOGLE ADSENSE & COOKIES

We partner with Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to nothingspace.in and other sites on the internet.

OPT-OUT: You may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy at policies.google.com.

4. CHILDREN'S INFORMATION

We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their online activity. Nothing Space does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13.
