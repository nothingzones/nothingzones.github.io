ABOUT US

ESTABLISHED 2024 • COMMUNITY DRIVEN

Welcome to Nothing Space, the premier open-source resource hub designed exclusively for the Nothing™ technology ecosystem. We are not just a website; we are a collective of developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts who believe in the philosophy of transparent technology.

Our platform serves as a centralized bridge between the official Nothing OS updates and the user community. Whether you own a Phone (1), Phone (2), or a CMF device, our goal is to enhance your experience without compromising security.

WHY WE STARTED

The Android ecosystem is fragmented. Finding the correct OTA Zip file, a compatible Cam Presets, Ringtones, or a wallpaper that perfectly fits the OLED punch-hole layout can be frustrating. Nothing Space was born to solve this. We aggregate, verify, and host resources so you don't have to scour broken forum links.

OUR VERIFICATION PROTOCOL

  • Tier 1 (Source): We verify that Firmware links originate directly from Nothing's official OTA servers.
  • Tier 2 (Scan): APKs and Tools are scanned using VirusTotal APIs to ensure zero malware presence.
  • Tier 3 (Testing): Our internal team tests modifications on physical devices to rule out bootloops or stability issues.
